❓ Help & FAQ
How do I join SeriConnects? ▼
Tap "Join For Free", fill your name, batch year and details, then tap Create Account. It's completely free!
Who can join? ▼
Only students and alumni of College of Sericulture, Chintamani. Both seniors (working) and juniors (students) are welcome!
How to find seniors for job help? ▼
Go to Directory tab and search by name, occupation or location. Or use Batch Wise tab to find seniors from specific years.
How to update my profile? ▼
Go to Profile tab (👤) and tap "Edit Profile". Update your occupation, location, mobile, Gmail and social media links.
Is this app free? ▼
Yes! SeriConnects is completely free for all students and alumni of College of Sericulture, Chintamani.
How to contact a senior/junior? ▼
Open their card in Directory. You'll see their mobile, Gmail, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn to connect directly.
